# CPC ASCII Maze Game
This game was born from the prototyping of my idea to make a new version of Sulans Maze
for the Amstrad CPC. It felt like it would make a good base for a Rouge-like maze game
so I have forked to this new directory.

You will get a choice of 255 procedrally generated mazes. Your energy will go down slowly 
with each move, but if you meet the ghost it will push you back and take 50 energy. 
You can not leave the maze until you have collected all the gems.

Keys are:
A and D to turn left and right.
W and S to move forward and backwards.
Q to quit
